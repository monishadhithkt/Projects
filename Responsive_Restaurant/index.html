ABOUT US

Introduction:

Welcome to PK Restaurant, your neighborhood restaurant for delicious and comforting food. We believe that the best dishes are the ones that remind you of childhood. A portion of great food should make you feel like you are wrapped in a warm blanket.

History:

Blanket Food was founded in 2023 by two friends who grew up eating comfort food together. They wanted to create a restaurant that would bring back those warm and fuzzy feelings of home.

Food:

Our menu features a variety of comfort food dishes, such as mac and cheese, fried chicken, and dumplings. We use only the freshest ingredients, and we take pride in our cooking. Our dishes are made with love, and we hope you will enjoy them as much as we do.

Experience:

We want you to have a memorable dining experience at Blanket Food. Our staff is friendly and attentive, and we are committed to providing you with the best possible service. We hope you will come back and visit us again soon!
